Everything you are ashamed of
所有让你羞赧的事情

all the parts of yourself that you keep secret
你身上所有想隐藏的秘密

everything you want to change about yourself
所有你想改变自己的地方

it is who you are
组成了你这个人

that is your power
这些都是你的力量

deny it and you are nothing
否认它们你将一无是处

There are some things success is not.
有些事并不代表着成功。

It’s not fame , it’s not money or power.
不是名誉，不是金钱或权利。

Success is waking up in the morning
成功是清晨醒来

So excited about what you have to do
对自己的人生充满期待

That you literally fly out the door
让你激动得飞出房门

It’s getting to work with people you love
是与你爱的人们一同拼搏

Success is connecting with the world and making people feel
成功是与世界为伴，感染周围的人

It’s finding a way to bind together people
是将人们凝聚到一起

Who have nothing in common but a dream
一起追逐梦想

It’s falling asleep at night knowing you did the best job you could
是夜晚入眠时的问心无愧

Success is joy and freedom and friendship
成功是喜悦、自由和友情

And success is love
成功是爱
