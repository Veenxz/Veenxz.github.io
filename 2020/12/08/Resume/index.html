PROFILE

I am a prospective senior student majoring in desertification combating. My research mainly encompasses evaluating and optimizing the protection system in the desertification area using CFD.

After getting in touch with some research, I have a great interest in environmental-related research, especially in the fluid mechanic fields. I focused on some research about biofluid mechanics in the respiratory system during COVID-19. So I will do some biofluid mechanics research at Huazhong University of Science and Technology next year. According to my background, I am glad to participate in some research on some fundamentals of fluid mechanics. Especially in fluid-structure interaction, multiphase flow, and solving methods.

CFD was applied widely in aerospace, but few about complex boundary layers flow. I desire to research these fields in my future career.

SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH

Mathematical Modeling and Computational Simulation of Turbulent Flows Around Shelterbelts (From 2019 Sep To 2021 June)

Advisor:
Associate Professor Guang-Lei Gao, School of Soil Water Conservation, Beijing Forestry University
Associate Professor Wei-Xi Huang, School of Aerospace Engineering, Tsinghua University

Overview and Achievements

  • Goal: Modeling the flow field around natural trees with CFD based on tree information and wind information collection.
  • Finished experiment design and literature research independently.
  • Our Work: Using OpenCV processing images to calculate aerodynamic porosity of natural trees. Finished wind information collection and processing of Hailaer and Zhangwu in 46a. Porosity model can be successfully modeled with current setting in fluent.
  • Prospect: Building tree geometry model using machine learning and modeling flow field using OpenFOAM in further research.
  • Brief Comment: Compared with previous research, our geometry model is more close to natural tree, our work is the first CFD flow field analysis around Pinus sylvestris var. mongolica in the world. This research is of great significance for the constitution of plantations.
